Project Details

Context: Starlife Assurance Company Limited

During my tenure as Junior Data Officer and National Service Personnel at Starlife Assurance, I was responsible for managing premium administration, client ledger analysis, and financial reporting. This experience provided deep insights into insurance operations and financial data management.

Challenge

  • Manage over 500 active policy records with multiple premium payments
  • Identify discrepancies between recorded and paid premiums
  • Process monthly commission calculations for institutional clients
  • Ensure data accuracy for regulatory compliance and audits
  • Generate timely reports for client and management review

Solution

I developed a comprehensive Excel-based system that automated much of the manual work:

  • Premium Ledger Dashboard: Real-time view of all premiums, payments, and balances
  • Reconciliation Tools: Automated matching of recorded vs. paid amounts
  • Commission Calculator: Formula-based commission computation with audit trail
  • Payment Tracker: Track collection status and follow-up dates
  • Exception Reports: Automatic flagging of unusual transactions for review

Excel Features & Techniques

Advanced Formulas Used

VLOOKUP - Match customer data across multiple sheets
INDEX/MATCH - Dynamic lookups with multiple criteria
SUMIFS - Conditional summation for ledger balancing
COUNTIFS - Track payment frequencies and patterns
IF/NESTED IF - Complex decision logic for payment status
PIVOT TABLES - Multi-dimensional analysis and reporting
CONDITIONAL FORMATTING - Visual anomaly detection
DATA VALIDATION - Prevent invalid data entry

Dashboard Components

  • Summary Dashboard: High-level metrics for management review
  • Customer Ledger Sheet: Detailed transaction history per policy
  • Payment Schedule: Due dates and collection status
  • Commission Tracking: Calculate and track commissions earned
  • Exception Log: Discrepancies and anomalies requiring investigation
  • Audit Trail: Track all changes with timestamps and user info

Industry Best Practices Applied

  • Audit trails - Complete documentation of all changes with timestamps
